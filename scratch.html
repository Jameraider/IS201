Take-aways from this Data

This is data from strong-men competitions, i.e. competitions of people who essentially make moving weight their career. Ronnie Coleman was not a strong man, he was a bodybuilder. However even though the focus of his training was to put on as much mass as possible, his lifts were competitive with strong mens. The average deadlift from strong men was 272 kg, with Ronnies being at 363 kg. The pattern continues with squats. Ronnies strength was vastly above that of those in a sport he didnt even compete in.
